About Delhi

Delhi, India’s capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area in the country’s north.
In Old Delhi, a neighborhood dating to the 1600s, stands the imposing Mughal-era Red Fort, a symbol of India, and the sprawling Jama Masjid mosque, whose courtyard accommodates 25,000 people. Nearby is Chandni Chowk,
a vibrant bazaar filled with food carts, sweets shops and spice stalls.

What is special about delhi

The distinguished city of Delhi is blessed with several gardens and botanical houses which are again one of the features that Delhi is famous for. The Garden of Five senses, the Lodi Gardens, the Buddha Jayanti Park and Nehru Park are some of these gardens which have made Delhi famous among nature lovers.
